About Us

Our Roots

SRK Restaurants was founded on the vision of celebrating the diverse culinary traditions of India. Our journey began in the heart of India, where we meticulously curated an array of regional dishes, each boasting its own unique blend of spices and cultural influences. This commitment to honoring the heritage of Indian cuisine has been our guiding principle as we've expanded our presence worldwide.









A Global Presence:

Today, SRK Restaurants spans the globe, with a network of fine-dining establishments in major cities across North America, Europe, Asia, and beyond. Each restaurant is a culinary oasis that captures the essence of India's culinary diversity, from the fiery curries of the South to the delectable tandoori delights of the North.








Our Menu:

Our extensive menu caters to a wide range of tastes and preferences. From timeless classics like butter chicken and biryani to contemporary creations that blend tradition with innovation, SRK Restaurants offers a culinary journey that caters to both newcomers and connoisseurs of Indian cuisine.








Join Us on a Flavorful Journey:

At SRK Restaurants, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey that transcends borders. Whether you are seeking the comfort of familiar flavors or the thrill of new taste sensations, we promise to deliver an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the best of Indian cuisine.

Come dine with us and discover why SRK Restaurants has become a global destination for those who seek the authentic flavors and warm hospitality of India. Join us in savoring the essence of Indian cuisine, one delectable bite at a time.
